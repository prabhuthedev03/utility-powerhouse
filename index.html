Why Test Your Hardware?

Whether you are a professional gamer or a remote worker, hardware latency and "ghosting" can ruin your productivity. This tool allows you to verify that every click and keystroke is registered instantly by your browser.

How to fix Keyboard Ghosting?

If you notice some keys aren't lighting up when pressed together, your keyboard may lack "N-Key Rollover." Upgrading to a mechanical keyboard can solve this.

Note for 2026 Users: Always ensure your browser has permission to access input devices for the most accurate polling rate results.
